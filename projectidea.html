Roles

There are many roles that need to be assigned in order to get a proper website out there, like website developer, marketing strategist, UX designer, lead developer, lead designer, you name it! (Wahhab 2016 & Diffily 2013). However, while working on our project, Honest Green Lion did not really assign any specific roles. All the members did what they felt most comfortable with doing. Ayesha was assigned the task of designing the website, which she accomplished using Adobe XD. Matthew has worked on presenting our idea to potential employers/ venture capitalists, and the rest of the members worked on the deliverables for A3 and A5. HGL has yet, not found the time to begin working on the development of the actual website, however, we do plan to work on it, so hopefully soon enough, we will have something up and running!
