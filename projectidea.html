AutomobileShare

Topic

AutomobileShare is the project idea of Honest Green Lion. AutomobileShare is a web-based service that allows car owners to list their vehicles with availability for individuals to hire from a few hours to days or weeks. Individuals can search for car brands, availability and location. This is intended to give individuals an efficient experience. There are countless websites from car rentals to share rides such as Kayak, and Autorentals.com, etc (Perkins 2021), however Honest Green Lion looked at this idea and thought why not allow people to make money off their own vehicles. Users will be able to choose a location to pick up the vehicle, have a timeframe the vehicle is available, and set prices depending on what time of the day it is or what day of the week. AutomobileShare is a different concept to the traditional rental of a car. Members of this service have total control over every aspect of this service.
Members will be able to sign up to this service via a website or mobile app. Members will need to provide personal data such as Name, Date of Birth, licence type, state of Issue, any state, territory or federal convictions (such as drink driving, loss of licence offences) (FTC.gov 2012). This data will then be vetted to confirm the member is suitable for this service at this current time. Users of this service will also need to bear in mind if they are involved in an accident an excess of the members vehicle will need to be paid, this will ensure the members who offer their car to this service aren't out of pocket and users cannot treat this service as a free for all.

Motivation

Could your car make you money? Does your vehicle just sit in the garage? Do you worry about upcoming expenses for your vehicle? What if there was a way you could make your vehicle work for you. AutomobileShare may be the answer to your problems. AutomobileShare is a car sharing platform that will allow you to lease out your vehicle when it is not in use. Think of Airbnb but for vehicles. You will be able to set dates for your vehicle’s availability, set prices, set age limits and even approve or disapprove users from your vehicle. You will be 100% in control.

AutomobileShare a website where individuals who own vehicles can list them on the website to allow others to hire it for a few hours or days to earn the car owners some extra money. With the current trend in IT where access to all online listings can be found in one destination is what Honest Green Lion is aiming to get.

Plans and Progress

In Assignment two, Honest Green Lion have decided for our IT project to be a car rental service that is different to traditional rental car companies or ride share, it is called AutomobileShare (Bolotyukh 2020). This is different to other similar car hire or leasing companies as AutomobileShare allows users to sign up at the AutomobileShare website which has a clean and user-friendly interface with high signal-to-noise ratio (Bradley 2010). Users can determine all aspects of their hiring period, such as allowing users to make money off their own personal vehicles, users can also select their pickup locations, set the time frames as to when the vehicle will be available, and can set the costs for hiring during different days of the week or based on the time frame desired and possibly the number of kilometers driven. Users of AutomobileShare will have to remember that if they are involved in an accident that they will need to pay an excess amount that is deemed suitable to the owner of the vehicle.

For consumers to use AutomobileShare they must provide their general information such as name, date of birth (DOB), Address, License details; license type or class, license number, state, or territory of issue, expiry dates, any criminal records or convictions held, such as loss of license, disqualification, previous vehicle accidents, driving history checks or drunk or drug driving (Rent My Car 2021). This information will then be used to determine whether the user is suitable to use AutomobileShare. Upon sign up to AutomobileShare users will be notified that if they currently hold a probationary license, that they will be restricted to certain types of vehicles that are not high powered (like vehicles with a V8 engine) or any modified performance vehicles (like vehicles with supercharge). (More cars to choose from for P plate drivers, 2021). Those users who currently hold a learner’s driver license will be notified that they are unable to use and sign up AutomobileShare until they obtain their probationary license.

Vehicle owners will be able to set the costs for the hire of their vehicle based on what they believe is reasonable. The vehicle must have a full tank of fuel prior to being leased and must also be returned at the end of the lease with a full tank of fuel (Do I Need to Fill the Car with Petrol Before I Return It?, 2021). It is the vehicle owner’s responsibility to notify the person booking the car of the type of fuel required for their vehicle. Before any keys are passed over to the leaser, both the leaser and the vehicle owner will have to agree to the costs involved, they may be able to negotiate the prices for longer hire periods, which is at the discretion of the vehicle owner. The owner must also advise the users of any access costs that they will incur if any damage has been made to their vehicle. A lease agreement stating the exact hire details along with the cost involved must be signed by both parties prior to keys being handed over (The Difference Between a Lease and a Rental Agreement, 2021). At completion of the agreement all funds required will be pre-approved, to ensure that the funds are available. Once the hire period has ended and the keys are returned to the owner, the owner must then check the vehicle, to see if any damage has been made, the fees will then be processed and deposited into the nominated bank account.

As a requirement for all members of AutomobileShare who wish to share their vehicle must provide evidence of recent full-service history of the vehicle along with a copy of registration details; to ensure that the vehicle is roadworthy and registered prior to the vehicle being available to consumers for hire (Opening a Car Hire Company: Top 3 Legal Issues| LegalVision, 2021). Along with these above-mentioned requirements and for vehicle owners and consumers peace of mind and to ensure the safety of all members all vehicles must also have full comprehensive insurance. Vehicles can be fitted with GPS (Global Positioning System) tracking devices attached to them so along with AutomobileShare the owners of the vehicle will be able to always see the location of their vehicle (When you rent a car, does the company secretly track you?, 2021). Owners of the vehicles may choose to pass on some of the costs of, vehicle maintenance, GPS tracking devices, full comprehensive insurance, registration etc. to the users.

If members of AutomobileShare have not been accurate or honest with their vehicle details or requirements, such as stating that their vehicle has full comprehensive insurance, or recent full service or GPS tracking than they will be held accountable for any issues that may arise and AutomobileShare will not be held responsible or accountable for this. Upon sign up of members to the AutomobileShare website or App, a legal document stating these terms and conditions must be signed prior to any hiring agreements of the vehicle taking place (Insurance Law Service, 2021).

All users must abide by all road rules and regulations throughout their hiring period. If any illegal behavior is undertaken by the leaser during this time, they may be required to forfeit the vehicle and be banned from using AutomobileShare services again. They will also be held accountable to pay for any fines, or infringement notices that they may have incurred during their hire period (10 Things Not to Do When Renting a Car, 2021).

AutomobileShare members will have the ability to have contactless pick-up and drop off services available for their vehicles via setting allocated locations with the vehicles key being kept in lockers. Once suitable users have been determined after all their personal information has been verified, they will then be notified of the location they can pick up the key for their selected vehicle or the location and time they can meet with the owner to pick up the key (Ultimate Guide to Renting a Car for the First Time | AutoSlash, 2021).

Once the vehicle leaser has finished their desired hiring period, they will be prompted to review their experiences, give feedback, and rate the services from AutomobileShare. Once this is completed as an incentive for uses to come back and use AutomobileShare again they may receive a discount that would expire within a certain time frame to use on another rental agreement (How rental car companies reach high customer satisfaction, 2021).

To make sure that our project is successful once it is launched, we will conduct some usability testing (Praxent 2019). This will be done by completing wire-framing and interactive prototyping for the website, AutomobileShare, and finding potential users, and conducting interviews. Users that book/ rent cars online were considered as potential users for our website. We conducted interviews and usability testing from five potential users, and the design solution was patched up accordingly. We have begun working on the website. Risks

For our project, we found there to be three major risks. One risk to take into consideration are the tools that we have assigned necessary for future use. Not all members of the group have had experience with IT before and therefore most of these tools are relatively new to them. At this stage there is no way of knowing how much difficulty each member will have learning the software. Another risk is using the limited time allocated to develop such an extensive application. Many components of the application were easy to construct but as we further prepare our project for presentation to the stakeholders, perfecting certain components take time we do not have. According to Barrow (2018) ‘applications can take anywhere between three and nine months to develop’. However, we only had 15 weeks to finalise everything. The final risk recognised for this project was only focusing on a single platform for our application. There are many advantages to this such as cost efficiency (Perera n.d). However, developing the app for use in only a web browser means that accessing the website on a handheld device, such as a mobile phone, may exhibit varying results such as the display not being as flawless as on a computer (Perera n.d). People want to access a user-friendly application on mobile when a laptop is inaccessible and if there are problems with the website may choose to download an alternate app instead.
